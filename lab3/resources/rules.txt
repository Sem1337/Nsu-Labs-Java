The player opens the cells, trying not to open the cell with the mine. 
Opening a cell with a mine, he loses. 
Mines are placed after the first turn.
Limitations on the size of the field:
lines: from 1 to 50
columns: 1 to 50
number of mines: from 0 to (rows * columns-1)
commands: 'exit' 'retry' 'settings' 'highscores'  'about' 'open x y'  'flag x y'
